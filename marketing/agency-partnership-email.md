# Agency Partnership Email — Co-Referral Outreach

Use this to reach Canadian SEO, branding, and marketing agencies who need a dev partner.
Send to 10 agencies per week. Target: build 3–5 active referral partners.

---

## Email Template

**Subject:** Dev partner for your clients? (Ontario-based, co-refer)

> Hi [Name],
>
> I'm Ronak from ElevenClicks — we're an Ontario-based dev shop building web apps, mobile apps, and AI solutions for Canadian businesses.
>
> I noticed [Agency Name] does great work on the [SEO / branding / paid ads / design] side. We often run into the opposite problem — clients who need marketing help after we build their site. I imagine you sometimes hit clients who need custom development that's outside your scope.
>
> Would you be open to a simple co-referral arrangement? We'd send you any clients who need your services, and if you ever have a client who needs custom dev work, we'd love to be your first call. We pay a 10% referral fee on any project that closes from your intro.
>
> No paperwork, no complicated agreements — just a handshake intro when the fit is right.
>
> Worth a 20-minute call to see if it makes sense?
>
> Ronak
> ElevenClicks — Ontario IT Company
> elevenclicks.com | info@elevenclicks.com

---

## Who to Target

| Search term | Why they're a good fit |
|---|---|
| "SEO agency" Toronto / Ottawa / Calgary | Their clients always need faster, better-built sites |
| "branding agency" Ontario | They design but can't build — perfect handoff |
| "digital marketing agency" Canada | Run paid ads but clients need landing pages built |
| "Shopify agency" Canada | Need Rails/Node dev for anything custom |
| "UX design studio" Ontario | Design without dev — natural partnership |

---

## Process

1. Find 10 agencies per week on LinkedIn or Google
2. Personalise one line — mention something specific about their agency
3. Send the email above
4. Follow up once after 7 days if no reply:

> "Hey [Name], just bumping this in case it got buried. No worries if the timing's off — happy to revisit whenever it makes sense."

5. For anyone who replies positively — book a 20-min call, confirm the co-refer arrangement verbally, done.

---

## Notes

- One active referral partner can send 3–5 deals per year
- 10% referral fee on closed projects is the standard in Canada
- No formal contract needed for the first few referrals — build trust first
- Best partners are agencies who are constantly being asked "can you also build this?" by their clients
